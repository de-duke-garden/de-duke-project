## 1. Acceptance of These Terms

By creating an account or using the De-Duke mobile app or website ("the Platform"), you agree to

## 2. What De-Duke Is (and Isn't)

De-Duke is a marketplace platform that connects **Guests** with **Hosts/Owners** and
**Agents/Agencies** for Commercial property sales/leases and Shortlet bookings.

**De-Duke is not a party to the underlying sale, lease, or booking transaction between a Guest and
a Host/Agency.** We provide the platform, payment infrastructure, verification process, and support
chat that make the transaction possible, but the transaction itself is between the Guest and the
Host/Agency. De-Duke's own role, and the limits of that role, are described throughout this
document.

## 3. Accounts and Roles

- **Guests** browse, search, book, and pay for listings.
- **Hosts** list Commercial or Shortlet properties after completing verification for one of six
  host types: **Owner** (photo + bio only), **Agent**, **Company**, **Lawyer**, **Architect**, or
  **Surveyor** (each requiring type-specific supporting documents, reviewed manually by De-Duke
  staff before a "Verified Host" badge is granted).
- **Agencies** are a Host variant supporting a team of agents managing a shared portfolio.

You must provide accurate information when creating your account and completing verification.
Submitting false, forged, or misleading verification documents is grounds for immediate account
termination and may be reported to relevant authorities.

You are responsible for safeguarding your account credentials and for all activity under your
account.

## 4. Fixed Pricing — No In-App Negotiation

Every listing on De-Duke is offered at a single, fixed price. De-Duke does not provide, and does
not intend to provide, an in-app mechanism for offers, counter-offers, or price negotiation. If a
Host wishes to change a listing's price, they must edit the listing itself; the price shown to a
Guest at the time they create a booking hold is the price that applies to that transaction.

## 5. Booking Holds

Before payment, a Guest reviews a confirmation summary (listing, price, dates) and must explicitly
confirm. Confirming creates a time-limited **hold** (default 15 minutes) on the listing for the
selected dates/possession period, during which no other Guest can hold or book overlapping dates.
If payment is not completed within the hold window, the hold expires automatically and the
listing/dates become available again. Holding a listing does not guarantee a completed transaction
and does not obligate De-Duke or the Host to any particular outcome beyond the hold period itself.

## 6. Payments, Commission, and Payouts

Payment processing is provided by **Paystack**. By making a payment on De-Duke, you also agree to
Paystack's own applicable terms for that transaction.

- **Commission:** De-Duke charges a **buyer fee** (added to the listing price, paid by the Guest)
  and an **owner commission** (deducted from the listing price, borne by the Host/Agency) on every
  completed transaction. Both rates are configured independently by De-Duke and may change from
  time to time; a rate change only applies to transactions initiated after the change — it never
  changes the rate that applied to a transaction already in progress. Current rates are disclosed
  to you before you confirm a booking hold (Guest) or before your listing goes live (Host).
- **Escrow:** Funds paid by a Guest are held by De-Duke in escrow until a De-Duke Admin manually
  reviews and releases them to the Host/Agency's De-Duke wallet. This manual release step exists on
  every transaction — there is no automatic or time-based release.
- **Wallet & Withdrawal:** Once funds are released to a Host/Agency's wallet, the Host/Agency may
  request a withdrawal to their verified bank account at any time; withdrawals are processed
  automatically via Paystack's Transfer API with no further De-Duke approval step required at that
  stage, because the underlying funds already passed the manual escrow-release checkpoint.
- Full detail on fees, refunds, and payout timing is set out in our separate **Payment Terms**
  document, which forms part of these Terms of Service by reference.

## 7. The Three-Way Support Chat

De-Duke provides a real-time chat connecting a Guest, the relevant Host/Agency, and De-Duke
staff/admin. This chat exists for **communication, support, and dispute mediation** — it is not a
price-negotiation channel, and messages sent in it do not modify a listing's fixed price or any
transaction's terms. De-Duke staff may view and participate in any conversation for support,
verification confirmation, and dispute-mediation purposes. Attempting to arrange payment outside
the Platform after connecting via De-Duke's chat is discouraged and may violate these Terms (see
Section 9).

resolution. De-Duke's mediation decisions are made in good faith based on available information but
do not constitute legal adjudication of the underlying dispute between Guest and Host/Agency.
